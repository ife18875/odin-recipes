Puff-Puff

DESCRIPTION

Puff-Puff is a popular Nigerian snack made from deep-fried dough. It's a favorite at parties and street food stalls.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cups of water
  • Oil for deep frying

STEPS

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, yeast and salt together.
  2. Add warm water gradually, stirring until you get a smooth batter.
  3. Cover and let it rise for about 1hr until doubled in size
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Scoop batter with a spoon and drop into hot oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown while turning occasionally.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or any toppings of your choice.
  8. Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve.
