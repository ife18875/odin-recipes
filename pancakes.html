DESCRIPTION

This is a recipe for the perfect pancakes to make for breakfast/brunch. They are quick to make and can be served with toppings of your choice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 table spoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Butter or oil fot cooking

STEPS

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, egg and melted butter well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently. Don't overmix.
  4. Heat a lightlygreased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or any toppings of your choice.
