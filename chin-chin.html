Chin-Chin

DESCRIPTION

Chin Chin is a crunchy, sweet Nigerian snack made from fried dough.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Oil for frying

STEPS

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg and salt in a bowl.
  2. Rub in the butter until mixture looks crumbly.
  3. Add eggs and milk, mixing to form a dough.
  4. Roll dough on a floured surface and cut into small squares or strips.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels and allow to cool before serving.
